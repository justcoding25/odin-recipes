Red Velvet Cake Recipe

Description

This simple to make and vibrant coloured cake recipe is guaranteed to be a hit at your next Fourth of July party.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 cup of shortening
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon of vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Parchment paper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and shift flour to ensure a moist cake.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees oF for ten minutes. Lightly grease your 9-inch baking pan with cooking spray and line with parchment paper.
  3. Firstly, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, cocoa, baking soda , and salt in a large bowl. Add eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and vegetable oil; mix using an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.
  4. Then finally pour in the boiling water. The batter will be runny and thin but don't worry, I promise you it will come out amazing
  5. Pour into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for about half an hour, or until a clean toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
  7. Bring out of the oven and allow to cool in the pan for ten minutes.
  8. Gently bring the cake out of the pan, by tapping on the bottom, unto a cooling rack.
  9. Allow to cool for at least an hour or more before frosting or serving.
