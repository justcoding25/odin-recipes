Red Velvet Cake Recipe

Description

This simple to make and vibrant coloured cake recipe is guaranteed to be a hit at your next Fourth of July party.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 cup of shortening
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon of vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Parchment paper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and shift flour to ensure a moist cake.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees oF for ten minutes. Lightly grease your 9-inch baking pan with cooking spray and line with parchment paper.
  3. Beat 1 1/2 cups sugar and shortening together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time. Combine red food coloring and cocoa to make a paste; add to creamed mixture.
  4. Mix buttermilk, salt, and 1 teaspoon vanilla together in a small bowl. Add flour, alternating with buttermilk mixture, mixing just until incorporated. Mix vinegar and baking soda together. You sould see it bubbly and frothy.
  5. Then gently fold mixture into the batter and pour into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for about fourty-five minutes, or until a clean toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
  7. Bring out of the oven and allow to cool in the pan for ten minutes.
  8. Gently bring the cake out of the pan, by tapping on the bottom, unto a cooling rack.
  9. Allow to cool for at least an hour or more before frosting or serving.
