Vanilla Cake Recipe

Description

This is the best recipe if your looking for a quick desert to spice up that dinner party you forgot about. Moist, tasty ,and most of all cheap, this recipe is the favourite go-to of anyone craving something sweet.

Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cups of milk
  • Parchment paper
