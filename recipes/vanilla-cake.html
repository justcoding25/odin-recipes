Vanilla Cake Recipe

Description

This is the best recipe if your looking for a quick desert to spice up that dinner party you forgot about. Moist, tasty ,and most of all cheap, this recipe is the favourite go-to of anyone craving something sweet.

Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cups of milk
  • Parchment paper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees oF for ten minutes. Lightly grease your 9-inch baking pan with cooking spray and line with parchment paper.
  2. Firstly, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, vanilla extract, and salt in a large bowl. Add eggs, milk, and vegetable oil; mix using an electric mixer on low speed until smooth. Pour into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake for about an hour and half, or until a clean toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
  4. Bring out of the oven and allow to cool in the pan for ten minutes.
  5. Gently bring the cake out of the pan, by tapping on the bottom, unto a cooling rack.
  6. Allow to cool for at least an hour or more before frosting or serving.
