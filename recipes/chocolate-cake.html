Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description

Moist, decadent and absolutely scrumptous. This delicious and easy to prepare chocolate cake is a guaranteed birthday party hit.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cup of white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 1 cup of boiling water
  • Parchment paper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and shift flour to ensure a moist cake.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees oF for ten minutes. Lightly grease your 9-inch baking pan with cooking spray and line with parchment paper.
  3. Beat 1 1/2 cups sugar and shortening together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time. Combine red food coloring and cocoa to make a paste; add to creamed mixture.
  4. Mix buttermilk, salt, and 1 teaspoon vanilla together in a small bowl. Add flour, alternating with buttermilk mixture, mixing just until incorporated. Mix vinegar and baking soda together; gently fold into cake batter and pour into prepared pans.
  5. Then finally pour in the boiling water. The batter will be runny and thin but don't worry, I promise you it will come out amazing
  6. Pour into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake for about half an hour, or until a clean toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
  8. Bring out of the oven and allow to cool in the pan for ten minutes.
  9. Gently bring the cake out of the pan, by tapping on the bottom, unto a cooling rack.
  10. Allow to cool for at least an hour or more before frosting or serving.
